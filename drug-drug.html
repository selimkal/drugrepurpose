This website is developed to accompany the results from our TÜBİTAK-2236 project with the title: "A Computational Framework for Systematic Estimation of Drug Repurposing Opportunities based on Integrative Biological Knowledge Graphs".

In this study, we developed a computational framework to systematically estimate drug repurposing opportunities by integrating biological evidence dispersed in multiple public data repositories.
